What Is Baptism?

A public declaration of your decision to follow Jesus. You’ll be immersed in water by a pastor or a friend, symbolizing Jesus’ death and resurrection. Afterward, we celebrate with prayer and cheering from your church family.

[Icon: Dove]

Why Baptism?

Baptism symbolizes Jesus’ death, resurrection, and your new life in Him. Going underwater represents your sins being washed away; rising up pictures your fresh start.

[Icon: Water]

Jesus’ Mission

“Go and make disciples, baptizing them in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.” — Matthew 28:19

It’s a step in following Jesus.

How, When & Next Steps

Baptism happens during a Sunday service at MBC. Your church family will support you with prayer and encouragement.

What to Bring

  • Towel
  • Change of clothes
  • Bag for wet clothes

Wear suitable clothing (not see-through when wet). Flexible options are available if you’re anxious about water or speaking.
